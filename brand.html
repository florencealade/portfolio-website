Icon Set

This semester I developed a brand for myself. Part of the task was to create a fifth element for my brand. I decided to create an icon set that could go along with my brand and be used for various applications. Luckily I had a bit of practice in iconograpgy due to a project in IXD104. While creating my icons I wanted them to be simple yet still appear visually appealing to the viewer. I also had to consider my tone of voice and make sure that the design was in line with my brand.
